Learning objectives
After studying this chapter, you should:
• understand the tests for establishing UK residence;
• know the tax implications of residence status on income tax and capital gains tax (CGT),
and long-term residence status on inheritance tax (IHT);
• understand when an individual will be a qualifying new resident in the UK, and how this
will impact on their liability to income tax and CGT;
• be aware of the impact of the self assessment system on individuals who are not resident
in the UK; and
• be aware of double taxation relief for income tax, CGT and IHT.
